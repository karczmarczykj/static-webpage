# About Us

## Our Mission

We are dedicated to improving the quality of life for people living with diabetes. Our mission is to provide high-quality products, reliable information, and a supportive community for everyone affected by diabetes.

## Who We Are

Founded in 2020 by a team of healthcare professionals and diabetes advocates, our store was born from a personal understanding of the challenges faced by people with diabetes. Our founder was diagnosed with Type 1 diabetes at age 12 and has spent decades learning what works — and what doesn't.

Today, our team includes:
- **Endocrinologists** who review all health content
- **Nutritionists** who develop our meal plans and recipes
- **Certified Diabetes Educators** who are available for consultation
- **People with diabetes** who test every product we sell

## Our Products

We carefully select every product in our store. Our criteria:
1. **Clinical effectiveness** — backed by research
2. **Ease of use** — tested by our community
3. **Value for money** — fairly priced quality products
4. **Safety** — certified and approved

## Community

We believe that living well with diabetes is easier when you're not alone. Join our community through:
- **Blog** — regular articles from our team of experts
- **Newsletter** — weekly tips and product updates
- **Social media** — daily inspiration and community stories
