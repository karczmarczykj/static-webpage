## Returns Policy

### 30-Day Return Guarantee

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy for any reason, you may return most items within **30 days** of delivery.

### Conditions for Returns

- Item must be in original condition
- Original packaging must be intact
- For hygiene reasons, opened medical devices cannot be returned
- Food items cannot be returned once delivered

### How to Return

1. Contact us at returns@example.com with your order number
2. We will send you a prepaid return label
3. Package the item securely and drop off at any post office
4. Refund will be processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return

### Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Take a photo of the damage
2. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
3. We will send a replacement at no cost
