# Insulin Pump Waist Belt

Stay comfortable and stylish while keeping your insulin pump secure with our specially designed waist belt.

## Design Philosophy

We believe that managing diabetes shouldn't get in the way of living your life. The Insulin Pump Waist Belt was designed with input from insulin pump users to create the most comfortable and practical solution available.

## Features

- **Secure pocket** — your pump stays in place no matter what you're doing
- **Discreet profile** — worn under clothing, no one needs to know
- **Quick access** — designed for easy one-handed access when needed
- **All-day comfort** — breathable fabric prevents irritation

## Care Instructions

Machine wash cold, gentle cycle. Do not tumble dry. Air dry flat.
