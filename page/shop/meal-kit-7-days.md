# Diabetic-Friendly Meal Kit (7 days)

Take the guesswork out of diabetes-friendly eating with our expertly curated 7-day meal kit.

## What You Get

Each kit includes:
- **7 breakfasts** — yogurt parfaits, egg dishes, smoothie bowls
- **7 lunches** — salads, soups, wraps with low-GI fillings
- **7 dinners** — protein-rich mains with non-starchy vegetables

## Nutrition Goals

Every meal is designed to:
- Keep blood sugar levels stable
- Provide adequate protein (at least 20g per meal)
- Include plenty of fiber
- Stay within 30–45g carbohydrates per meal

## Delivery

Fresh ingredients are delivered Monday morning. All meals can be prepared in 30 minutes or less.
