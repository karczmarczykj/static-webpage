# Smart Glucose Monitor Pro

Experience the freedom of continuous glucose monitoring with the Smart Glucose Monitor Pro. No more finger pricks every time you want to check your levels — just scan the sensor worn on your arm.

## Key Features

The Smart Glucose Monitor Pro offers:
- **Continuous monitoring** — readings every 5 minutes
- **14-day sensor** — change your sensor just twice a month
- **Smart alerts** — get notified when levels are too high or too low
- **Trend data** — see where your glucose is heading, not just where it is

## How It Works

1. Apply the small sensor to your upper arm
2. Connect to the app on your smartphone
3. Monitor your glucose levels in real-time
4. Review trends and patterns to optimize your management
