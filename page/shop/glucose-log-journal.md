# Blood Pressure & Glucose Log Journal

Your comprehensive companion for tracking your health journey.

## Why Tracking Matters

Regular tracking of blood glucose, blood pressure, and other health metrics helps you and your doctor identify patterns, adjust medications, and make lifestyle changes that improve your health outcomes.

## Journal Contents

- **Daily pages** — morning/noon/evening glucose readings, blood pressure, weight
- **Meal diary** — what you ate and its estimated effect on your levels
- **Medication tracker** — doses and times
- **Notes section** — how you felt, activity levels, stress
- **Monthly charts** — visualize your trends over time

## About the Design

Crafted with input from endocrinologists and diabetes educators, this journal covers everything you need to track in a format that's easy to use every day.
