# Healthy Recipes for People with Diabetes

Eating well with diabetes doesn't mean giving up delicious food. Here are some of our favorite diabetes-friendly recipes.

## Breakfast: Greek Yogurt Parfait

**Ingredients:**
- 200g plain Greek yogurt (full fat)
- 50g mixed berries (blueberries, raspberries)
- 1 tablespoon chia seeds
- 1 tablespoon crushed walnuts
- Cinnamon to taste

**Instructions:**
1. Layer yogurt in a glass
2. Top with berries
3. Sprinkle chia seeds and walnuts
4. Add a pinch of cinnamon

**Nutritional info:** ~250 calories, 15g protein, 20g carbs

---

## Lunch: Salmon and Avocado Salad

**Ingredients:**
- 150g grilled salmon
- 1/2 avocado, sliced
- Mixed greens (spinach, arugula)
- Cherry tomatoes
- Cucumber
- Lemon juice and olive oil dressing

**Instructions:**
1. Place mixed greens in a bowl
2. Add salmon, avocado, tomatoes, and cucumber
3. Drizzle with lemon juice and olive oil
4. Season with salt and pepper

**Nutritional info:** ~400 calories, 32g protein, 12g carbs

---

## Dinner: Chicken and Vegetable Stir-Fry

**Ingredients:**
- 200g chicken breast, cut into strips
- 1 cup broccoli florets
- 1 bell pepper, sliced
- 1 cup mushrooms
- 2 cloves garlic
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce (low sodium)
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil

**Instructions:**
1. Heat sesame oil in a wok or large pan
2. Add garlic, cook 30 seconds
3. Add chicken, cook until done
4. Add vegetables, stir-fry 5-7 minutes
5. Add soy sauce, toss to combine

**Nutritional info:** ~350 calories, 40g protein, 18g carbs

*These recipes are designed to have minimal impact on blood sugar levels. Actual nutritional values may vary.*
